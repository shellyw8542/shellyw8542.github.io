Context

Mr. Mueller assigned the whole class to do a partner project about an adventure and create different scenarios using hyperlinks.

Content

My project was about making breakfast. I thought of some cool ideas like time travel, the life of a rubber duck, etc. But I wanted to keep it simple and easy for me to actually do and create without making too much scenarios. Mr Mueller organized it when my peers find something interesting towards themselves and then I got to work with my partner, Ramiz.

Reflection

Overall, this project really helped me understand a lot about how important communication is. This also helped me with coding my IDE and interacting with github better. I find that communication was really important during this project because my partner and I were struggling with the merge conflict and also doing the project itself. I wanted to do one part my partner did wanted to do the same part. I find the next step into working with another partner project would be to communicate and to fully plan things out.
